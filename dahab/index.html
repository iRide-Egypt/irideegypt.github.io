Dahab Ride

sea, mountains, cinematic

Dahab's ride is one of the best rides we offer. Dahab (South Sinai) is based on natural diversity. Our checkpoints are: Cairo, Suez tunnel and South Sinai. In Dahab, we ride on the seashore (The Red Sea) , between the rocky mountains, and in several beautiful places which ware kept as Bedouin secrets for years. This ride is special, as you can gallop freely between the mountains, swim with your horse on the seashore, see awesome places that nobody else can see if they don't go on a horseback, and finally take the best photos. We take full care of the transportation, accomodation, food and anything a rider may necessarily need. We also help our riders get involved in other activities such as diving, snorkling, dining, meditation, etc.

Duration

In Total: 3 days/2 nights
On Horseback: 3-6 hours

Prices

This event allows people to join with their relatives who are not able to ride horses or don't want to. We know that our prices are not the least in the market, and this is because we provide our riders with the best experience possible. Check our reviews on Facebook.

Eligibility

To join us you do not need any experience in horseback riding. We choose the best match for you depending on your level. If it is the first time for you to ride horses it won't be the last. And if you are a professional rider, then our professional horses are waiting for you.


Dahab's ride is flexible and suitable for families and individuals who look forward to having a vacation for more than 3 days on the seaside. We guarantee you that you will get back home with as less stress as a new born baby. If you are interested in this ride, please let us know by contacting us. All our strategies and conditions are applied to beginners and pros.
